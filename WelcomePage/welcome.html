Welcome to Link Bubble!

How does it work?
  1. Click a link in an app.
  2. A bubble will spawn on the edge of the screen which will load that link in the background. As soon as the link is ready, it will be displayed for you.


It's that simple. With Link Bubble, you can keep using your current app rather than wasting your time watching links redirect and waiting for pages to load. What's not to love about that?

Link Bubble does plenty of other cool stuff such as:

  • Redirecting the current page to a dedicated app (where appropriate).
  • Locating YouTube videos on webpages so you can easily load them in the YouTube app.


Link Bubble Pro

The free version gives you a taste of the Link Bubble's power, but if you want to load multiple bubbles at once or load links from all the apps on your system, you'll want to upgrade to Link Bubble Pro. As well as unlocking the full feature set of Link Bubble, going Pro also helps ensure I get to write more of these apps in the future :)
